Ube-Cheese Pandesal

Yield: 24 pcs.

Ingredients:

Dough:

  • 150 mL (130F) water
  • 3/4 cup + 1 TB white sugar
  • 2 tsp dry active yeast
  • 4 cup bread flour
  • 160 g fresh milk
  • 2 tsp McCormick Ube
  • 1 TB Flavorade Ube
  • 1/2 - 1 tsp salt (amount of salt depends on whether the butter is salted or unsalted)
  • 60 g butter (melted)
  • 1 pc large egg
  • 2 cups breadcrumbs (for coating)

Filling:

  • 3 1/4 cups ube halaya
  • 480 g cheese

Instructions:

Bowl 1

  1. In a bowl, melt 1 TB sugar in warm water.
  2. Add the dry active yeast and let it bloom.
  3. Add 1 cup of bread flour and mix. Cover and let rest for 10 mins.

Bowl 2

  1. While waiting, in a separate bowl, combine fresh milk, remaining white sugar, ube flavorings, salt, melted butter, and egg. Mix until fully combined.
  2. Add the remaining flour. Mix until fully combined.
  3. Once the first dough has rested for 10 minutes, combine the two doughs and put it in a bread maker.

Portioning and filling

  1. Divide the dough into 24 pieces (approx. 45g each) and form them into balls.
  2. Rest the dough for 30 mins.
  3. Flatten the ball and add 2 TB of ube halaya and 1 slice of cheese (approx. 20g). Close the dough, form it into a ball again, and roll it in breadcrumbs. Repeat for all the dough balls.
  4. Rest the dough for another 30 mins. While resting, preheat your oven to 175C.

Baking

  1. Bake in the oven for 20 mins.
